From The Sea To The Land Beyond

UPDATE: landbeyond.co.uk is now offline, the project having run its course.

For the past seven weeks I’ve been building landbeyond.co.uk. It’s a website promoting the film From The Sea To The Land Beyond, which is launching at Sheffield Doc/Fest. The film is made from archive footage of the UK’s coastline from the past hundred years, made available by the BFI, and features a soundtrack by British Sea Power.

On the site you can browse a selection of almost 300 archive clips and put them together along with British Sea Power’s music to make a sort of film postcard that incorporates the fun flippy-over technology that I discussed here, along with lots of HTML5 video and audio goodness.
